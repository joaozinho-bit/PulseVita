The device uses an infrared proximity sensor to read forehead temperature without direct skin contact, and an optical sensor in the finger area to detect your pulse. Both readings are processed and displayed on the integrated screen within about a second.

Turn it on, place your finger on the BPM pad at the bottom, and bring the top of the device near your forehead. The screen will show your temperature and heart rate, and the RGB lights will indicate whether your readings are within a normal range.
